    FAQ

    Home FAQ

    General

    Do you sell organic or free-range products? ▶

    Yes, we offer organic and free-range options upon request.

    Can I visit the farm or facility before placing a wholesale order? ▶

    Yes, we allow scheduled visits for businesses interested in wholesale partnerships.

    How can I contact customer service for wholesale inquiries? ▶

    You can reach us by phone, email, or through our website’s contact form.

    Ordering & Payment

    How do I place a wholesale order? ▶

    You can place an order through our website, via email, or by contacting our sales team directly.

    What are your payment terms for wholesalers? ▶

    We offer flexible payment options, including upfront payments and credit terms for approved clients.

    Is there a deposit required for large wholesale orders? ▶

    A deposit may be required for bulk or special orders. Our sales team will provide details.

    Meat and Poultry

    What types of meat do you supply? ▶

    We provide wholesale beef, lamb, chicken, and fish in various cuts and packaging options.

    Can I order whole animals instead of processed meat? ▶

    Yes, we offer whole animals for butchering according to your needs.

    Do you provide halal slaughtering certificates? ▶

    Yes, we can provide halal certification upon request.

    What is your process for ensuring meat quality? ▶

    We follow strict hygiene and safety measures, including government-approved processing facilities.

    Fruits and Vegetables

    What fruits and vegetables do you supply? ▶

    We supply a variety of fresh fruits, including apples, oranges, bananas, mangoes, watermelons, pineapples, and grapes. Seasonal fruits such as durians, berries, and melons are also available. Contact us for a full product list.

    Can I order imported fruits through your wholesale service? ▶

    Yes, We primarily focus on locally grown produce, but we may source imported fruits upon request.

    How do you ensure the freshness of your fruits and vegetables? ▶

    Our produce is harvested fresh and delivered promptly to maintain quality.

    Delivery and Logistics

    Do you offer delivery for wholesale orders? ▶

    Yes, we provide delivery services for bulk orders. Delivery fees may apply.

    What is the minimum order for delivery? ▶

    The minimum order varies by location. Contact us for details.

    Can I arrange my own transportation for pickup? ▶

    Yes, customers can arrange their own pickup from our farm or warehouse.
